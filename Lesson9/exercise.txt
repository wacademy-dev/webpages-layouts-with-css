<h3>Lesson 9 Exercise - Use the grid-template property</h3>
<p>Open the Lesson 9 folder of the <code>web-pages-layouts-with-css</code> Exercises repo you cloned for this course and carry out the following tasks.</p>
<p><strong>Tasks</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Find the&nbsp;<code>style.css</code>&nbsp;file, which is inside the&nbsp;<code>css</code>&nbsp;folder.</li>
<li>Locate the style rules for&nbsp;<code>.container</code>. You'll see a set of rules for&nbsp;<code>grid-template-columns</code>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<code>grid-template-rows</code>.</li>
<li>Combine the column and row specifications into one line of code using&nbsp;<code>grid-template</code>. Remember: the row specifications come first, followed a forward slash, followed by the column specifications.</li>
<li>Save the your work and open in a browser to see your changes.</li>
</ul>
