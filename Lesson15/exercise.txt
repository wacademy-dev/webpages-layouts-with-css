<h3>Lesson 15 Exercise - Align items and justify content</h3>
<p>Open the Lesson 15 folder of the <code>web-pages-layouts-with-css</code> Exercises repo you cloned for this course and carry out the following tasks.</p>
<p><strong>Tasks</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Find the&nbsp;<code>style.css</code>&nbsp;file, which is inside the&nbsp;<code>css</code>&nbsp;folder.</li>
<li>In&nbsp;<code>style.css</code>, find the&nbsp;<code>div</code>&nbsp;with a class of&nbsp;<code>container</code>.</li>
<li>Align items along the main axis (<code>justify-content</code>) using the value of your choice. It could be&nbsp;<code>flex-start</code>,&nbsp;<code>flex-end</code>,&nbsp;<code>center</code>,&nbsp;<code>space-between</code>, or&nbsp;<code>space-around</code>.</li>
<li>Save the your work and open in a browser to see your changes.</li>
<li>Align items along the cross axis (<code>align-items</code>) using the value of your choice. It could be&nbsp;<code>stretch</code>,&nbsp;<code>flex-start</code>,&nbsp;<code>flex-end</code>,&nbsp;<code>center</code>, or&nbsp;<code>baseline</code>.</li>
<li>Save the your work and open in a browser to see your changes.</li>
</ul>
